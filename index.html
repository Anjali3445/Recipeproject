Gulab Jamun



Ingredients

  • Suger
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Take some sugar and b0il it with water to make sugerwater
  2. After boiling,make crumps ball by mixing with bread with water
  3. Deep fry ball in oil at low flame.
  4. Put these ball in sugarcane water for 10 minutes and gulab jamun is ready.
